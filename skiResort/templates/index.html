Choosing a Ski Resort

There are several factors that may be important to a given person when choosing the ultimate ski resort destination. Some factors include slope offerings (variety), total skiing distance, price, and location. This interactive dashboard allows you to compare various ski resorts located in North America.

Using the map, you can see the location and variations in altitude for a given location within North America. Additionally, the size of the marker is determined based on the slope offering rating.

If you are interested in exploring the impact of two factors, you can use the interactive scatter plots. These plots allow you to see the relationship of adult lift price compared to altitude, resort size, slope variety and total available skiing distance.
